About

Clarity is the craft.

The secret sauce for any great communication is a creative concept that cuts through the noise — reducing information clutter to the precise essence of what's needed. That's what Duncan McCann has spent 30 years perfecting.

"When a company has a rough idea of where they want to go, Duncan gets to know their market implicitly and provides the steps to get their message out there."
— Andy McGrath, Director, Vision8 Ltd

ZZZZZZ, producer and strategist, Duncan has worked across the full communications spectrum — from thirty-second TV commercials to major government policy comms, from Tui Award-winning music projects to brand strategy for national corporates.

A former Deputy Mayor of Palmerston North, he brings rare dual fluency: the creative instincts of an advertising director and the governance intelligence of an elected official. He has successfully delivered strategic communications and production for Business and Government sectors across New Zealand, Australasia and the Pacific.

Duncan holds a Diploma in Creative Writing (Massey), a Diploma in Tikanga & Te Reo Māori (Te Wānanga o Aotearoa) and is an Accredited Resource Management Commissioner. He is a member of PRINZ — the Public Relations Institute of New Zealand.
